Environment variables required for email notifications:

SMTP_HOST=smtp.yourprovider.com
SMTP_PORT=587
SMTP_USER=your_user@example.com
SMTP_PASS=your_password
FROM_EMAIL=369 Fitness <no-reply@369fitnesswellness.com>
NOTIFY_TO=admin@369fitnesswellness.com

Copy these into a .env file at the project root.
